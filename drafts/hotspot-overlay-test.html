STRIKING ALLOY WHEELS.

The BMW X7 is available with a range of striking alloy wheel designs, from 21" to 23" in size. Each wheel design has been carefully crafted to complement the X7's commanding presence, adding a touch of sportiness and luxury to its exterior profile.

overlay-Soft-Close-Doors

SOFT-CLOSE DOORS.

The Soft-close Automatic function gently pulls each door to its lock when closed. This convenient feature adds a sense of refinement, ensuring the doors close smoothly and quietly.

overlay-Split-Adaptive-LED-Headlights

SPLIT ADAPTIVE LED HEADLIGHTS.

The distinctive split headlights combine an upper ultra-slim LED daytime running light with a lower main LED headlight unit. This unique design gives the BMW X7 an immediately recognisable look while providing excellent illumination. The Adaptive LED headlights with BMW Selective Beam non-dazzling technology adapt their range and distribution to suit the road conditions.

ROOF RAILS.

Satinated Aluminium roof rails in the body colour are fitted as standard, providing an elegant design accent and offering the option for mounting additional accessories such as roof boxes and cycle carriers for your adventures.
